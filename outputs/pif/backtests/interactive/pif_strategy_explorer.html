PIF Strategy Step-Through Explorer

Walk the backtest one filing at a time. Each click reveals the next report, the signals that became public, the trades the strategy made, and the resulting performance path until the next filing arrives.

The focus strategy drives the narrative and report panels. Overlay strategies are optional visual comparisons. `P2` is the closest thing we have to a disclosed-sleeve proxy for visible `PIF` public-equity behavior.

How To Use

1. Choose a focus strategy. 2. Pick any comparison overlays. 3. Press Next Filing to animate from one public report window to the next. 4. Use the signal, order, and holdings panels to understand exactly why the portfolio changed.
